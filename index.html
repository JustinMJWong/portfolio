An Entrepreneur

I started off my career as an entreprenuer, riding onto the wave of e-commerce. A tough journey, i had to learn everything on my own from designing website, fulfilling customer orders, sourcing supplies, negotiating for better contract terms, harnessing transactions data and managing quirky customers request. I reflected, it was an fruitful experience as i had learnt tenacity and be resourceful to navigate my way through any challenges, learning new things on my own. The entrepreneurship journey had also sparked my data career to understand the capability of data deeper.

Learning Content Analyst in Performance Management

I moved from entrepreneurship and joined Ernst & Young as a consultant in learning and performance management. Grinding daily on translating lessons identified into learning programs and designing interactive learning content (do scroll down to see some screenshots of my creative works). I had worked with different stakeholders to develop storyboards with learning outcomes and bringing those storyboards to "live" with interactive e-learning content such as interactive PDFs, e-books, learning videos, eNewsletters and setting it in a learning management system. In addtion, i have also facilitated workshops and classess for much advance learners. This was a start to produce learning content and collect learners' data.

-

Switching from designing learning content to lead a data analytics project, the learning content was to give a flavour of the bigger picture, which is to create data for performance analysis. Everything that the learners have interacted with the learning management system had been captured into a database, which i always called it the holy grail. The database was very capable to provide transactional insights of the learners and comparing it to the learners day to day performance. The analysis resonated how i segmented my customers data to different profiles. I was able to identify the learners into different profiles and predicted their results to tweak the storyboard to fit learners better. This was recognised as a key capability by my client, which the team and i had scaled to benefit more people.
